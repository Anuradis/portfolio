About me

My focus is to develop as Front-End Developer. I believe that the qualities I have – diligence, analytical mind, fast learning capability and goal - orientation are the foundation of this; I have had the opportunity to be part of a microservice architecture project that consisted of about 30 people – mutual understanding, the awareness that everyone is fighting for a common goal as well as clear communication were the key to success.

I believe anything can be overcome through commitment and hardwork. Always up for learning new things.
